Our Services

At Your Service

SCREEN PRINT & EMBROIDERY

Wight’s professionally screen prints and embroiders on t-shirts, sweatshirts, jackets, hats, uniforms and hundreds of different promotional products in their Hampden, Maine facility. Detailed logo design and custom art creation is also an in house service Wight’s provides. Utilize Wight’s trained artists in their screen print and embroidery departments to promote and brand your business name and message. Create custom logos with six color options in printing and 12 color options in embroidery to make your brand or image become recognized. Whether it is the corporate world or the sporting goods team market Wight’s has the ability to help you and your organization be successful and looking professional!

ATHLETIC UNIFORMS

Wight’s has established a quality reputation for designing, building and servicing the State of Maine’s athletic uniform market. With a full line up of brand name uniforms, Wight’s has the ability to provide excellent quality and options in all lines of team sports uniforms. Men’s, Women’s and youth styles are available at many different price ranges to meet your team’s needs and budget. Wight’s can offer virtually every uniform style in all Team Sports with multiple price points to fit your needs! Let Wight’s help outfit your athletes to make them feel great so they can play great!

SPORTING GOODS

Wight’s has been selling sporting goods since 1914. Wight’s has a rich tradition of providing a diverse product mix for all team sports as well as most individual sports. From a simple whistle to a complete athletic department’s budget, Wight’s can meet all of your needs. Game balls, Hard Goods, training devices and more allow Wight’s to fill any sporting goods order. Wight’s provides all the Sporting Goods equipment you and your teams will need to be successful. Wight’s has products for Every Sport, Every Season and Every Player!

FACILITY EQUIPMENT

Wight’s has the ability to sell and install all aspects of athletic facility improvements. Ceiling hung, wall-braced or portable backboards are all available. Let Wight’s provide you divider curtains, custom wall-padding, sideline chairs or designed scorers tables to enhance your school’s interior image. Wight’s can also create new locker room lay-outs with lockers and team benches. Modern day weight room upgrades are available to help your athletes gain an edge over their competition. Wight’s can also provide all of your outdoor field equipment needs. Goals, bleachers, player benches, netting, weather protection field tarps, foul poles, backstops, fence caps, padding, batting cages, blocking and tackle sleds, bases and all maintenance tools to take care of all your fields is available at Wight’s. Wight’s will sell and Install all the items you will need to make your athletic facilities stand out from all the others. Wight’s can help separate you from your competition with State of the Art Facility improvements!
